Introduction to formal learning with focus on language development, numeracy skills, and creative activities.

Preparation for primary school with emphasis on reading, writing, and mathematical skills, along with social and emotional development.

Comprehensive curriculum aligned with national standards, focusing on core subjects, critical thinking, and character development.
